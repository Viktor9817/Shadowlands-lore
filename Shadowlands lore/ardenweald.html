Ardenweald & the Dreaming Groves

Beneath Ardenweald's bioluminescent canopy, slumbering spirits draw anima from ancient trees before returning to life. The Night Fae safeguard this cycle, shepherding the wild gods, loa, and nature-bound souls entrusted to their care.

Ardenweald is a realm of twilight autumn, a mirror to the Emerald Dream's spring. After the Arbiter judges a soul worthy of rebirth through nature, the Night Fae enshrine it within dream-trees called tirna, nurturing the essence until it can awaken in the mortal realm once more.

The Winter Queen presides over the cycle. Each grove reveres a unique tirna—Tirna Vaal, Tirna Glain, Tirna Eas—ensuring balance even amid the drought that once starved Ardenweald of anima. Mortal devotees may embrace the Grace of Forms, adopting animal shapes to serve the forest eternally.

"We are the bridge between endings and beginnings. In Ardenweald, every leaf that falls feeds the tree that will bloom again." — The Winter Queen

Groves & Spirits

  • Tirna Vaal: A sanctuary of rebirthing seeds, tended by fae menders and grovetenders.
  • Hibernal Hollow: Resting place of loa and wild gods recovering from mortal sacrifice.
  • Dreamsong Fenn: Resonant glades where anima songs lull spirits into restorative slumber.
  • Glitterfall Basin: Cascading starlight nourishes spirits awaiting the call to awaken.

Night Fae Pillars

  • Purpose Rebirth

    Anima rejuvenates nature spirits, returning them to the living world.

  • Champion Winter Queen

    She adjudicates every rebirth and safeguards the balance of seasons.

  • Ritual Wild Hunt

    The Hunt defends Ardenweald from invaders and recovers errant anima.

  • Bond Emerald Dream

    Ardenweald mirrors the Dream, representing the autumn and winter of nature's cycle.
